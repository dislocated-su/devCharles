Week 1 (2022-11-14 to 2022-11-20)

This week was all about team introduction. We had our first practical and we decided on team name and team roles and set up the platforms like Github and Discord. The team worked on preparing questions for upcoming client interview using the product brief.

Week 2 (2022-11-21 to 2022-11-27)

We had the client meeting on the Thursday (2023/11/24) of this week and it was followed by starting work on website and other deliverables for the assessment like Requirements, Risk Management, and Method Selection. This week we took our initial steps of development and set up libgdx.

Week 3 (2022-11-28 to 2022-12-04)

We continued working on the deliverables and this week we finally started development work. Implementation began on Sunday (2023/12/04), the last day of the week. We set up the basic project structure and added some assets to the repo on the same day. We finished work for some the deliverables. These were, Requirements, Risk assessments and Method selection.

Week 4 (2022-12-05 to 2022-12-11)

This week our work was mainly on polishing up some of the deliverables and we continued implementation of the game, albeit at a slower pace. For the game, we worked on delivering the functionality of switching between multiple cooks and cleaning up some of the work. We also introduced several additional classes and created a way to change control of cooks and improved movement. We worked on the physics of the game with metres.

Week 5 (2022-12-12 to 2022-12-18)

The third week of implementation while was not as productive, we still worked solving various problems like window rendering at wrong resolution until it is resized and adding more assets.

Week 6 (2022-12-19 to 2022-12-25)

The 2nd half of this week involved the Christmas period, but we held several meetings discussing the codebase, attempting first static body creation.

Week 7 (2022-12-26 to 2023-01-01)

This week the implementation team worked on making movement damping less sensitive and did an overhaul of movement physics. For the first few days of this week, the team took a few days off for the holidays.

Week 8 (2023-01-02 to 2023-01-08)

This week our team members were quite busy preparing for the winter exams but for the time that we had set aside for this project, we started working on the deliverable pdfs again. Requirements and risk assessment eeded to be worked on again. We worked on viewpoint in the game and some more physics like collisions.

Week 9 [Exam Week] (2023-01-09 to 2023-01-15)

Week 10 (2023-01-16 to 2023-01-22)

This first week back after the exam we introduced some improvement by changing the movement logic. W e picked up our work on physics and implemented collision detection. Fixed some bugs and restarted implementation on full pace again.

Week 11 (2023-01-23 to 2023-01-29)

This penultimate week, we did final work on our website and added new pages. This was undoubtedly the busiest week of the project. We created an emergency plan and distributed the unfinished work among team members. We worked on the User Interface, food rendering, food cooking logic, light adjustments, and map refactoring. We also finished up work of creating customer entities and their steering behaviour. W e finished working on risk assessment and other deliverable pdfs. We did our final works on the game and started getting ready for submission.

Week 12 (2023-01-30 to 2023-02-01)

These last few days we walked through the project and started polishing up and adding final touches. We added all our work on the website and finally brought the assignment to a finish line.
